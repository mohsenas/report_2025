2025 Development Journey

A timeline of significant milestones and achievements throughout the year.

Q1: Foundation & Infrastructure

Established core AI infrastructure with gRPC services. Launched Trial Balance and Profit & Loss reporting for the Finance system.

Q2: Specialized AI & Assets

Implemented Sales and Purchase Approval Agents. Enhanced Asset Management with dynamic expressions and financial transaction posting.

Q3: Suppliers & Portals

Released the Supplier Portal with RFQ and PO management. Advanced OCR capabilities for bilingual document processing were integrated.

Q4: Ecosystem Optimization

Finalized ZATCA e-invoicing integration. Upgraded the entire platform to Angular 20 and implemented the audit logging service.
