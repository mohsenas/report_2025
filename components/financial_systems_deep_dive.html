4. Financial Systems & Reporting

The financial backbone of Nawras ERP has undergone a complete modernization, introducing advanced reporting engines and robust transaction management.

Reporting & Financial Statements

  • Automated Reports: High-performance Profit and Loss, Balance Sheet, Trial Balance, and Cash Flow statements.
  • Period Management: Integrated financial period trial balance with dynamic filtering by year/month.
  • Export Capabilities: Advanced Excel export for all major financial reports.

Core Financial Architecture

  • Ledger Management: Rebuilt Journal Entries and General Ledger modules with multi-currency and exchange handling.
  • Mapping Schemes: Comprehensive ledger mapping system with dynamic dimension support and activation flows.
  • Subsidiary Control: Enhanced Accounts Payable and Receivable subsidiaries with direct integration from logistics (GRN/Delivery).
  • Asset Management: Advanced depreciation tracking and financial transaction posting for organizational assets.

Transaction Intelligence

  • Background Processing: Implemented asynchronous transaction mapping for high-volume data handling.
  • Audit & Validation: Real-time debit/credit validation and integration with the global audit service for traceability.
