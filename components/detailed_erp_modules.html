2. Nawras ERP Core Modules

The core of Nawras ERP has been expanded to cover the entire value chain of enterprise operations, from initial customer inquiry to final product delivery and financial reconciliation.

2.1 Sales Management

Primary Objective: Optimizing customer lifecycle management and revenue analysis.

  • Sales Order & Quotation: Complete lifecycle management with versioning, professional conversion tools, and audit trails.
  • Rework & Returns: Specialized handling for defective products and customer returns, integrated with inventory adjustments.
  • Pricing Dynamics: Flexible "Prices by Customer" configuration and negotiated contract management.
  • Customer Portal Enhancements:
    • Order Tracker timeline with clear state visualization and progress history.
    • Read/unread tracking for customer engagement monitoring.
    • Automated item mapping for seamless order processing.
  • Analytics: Interactive dashboards for revenue trends, regional performance, and representative commission structures.

2.2 Procurement (Purchase)

Primary Objective: Supplier relationship optimization and cost control.

  • Suppliers Portal: A massive achievement providing:
    • Request for Quotation (RFQ) system with full lifecycle management.
    • Advance Ship Notice (ASN) with transport tracking and warehouse integration.
    • Supplier Item Dictionary for mapping customer items to supplier codes.
  • Operational Tools: Robust PO creation, requisition workflows, and AI-recommended "Proposed POs" to optimize stock levels.
  • Automation: Event-driven integration with the Warehouse system for real-time shipment synchronization.

2.3 Manufacturing & Production

Primary Objective: End-to-end production control and precision costing.

  • Production Lifecycle: Management of build orders, resource capacity monitoring, and automated execution/closing workflows.
  • Costing Engine: Real-time standard vs. actual cost calculations, including material variances and operation rates.
  • Foundation: Comprehensive definition of Work Centers, Machines, BOMs, and Routing Codes.

2.4 Inventory & Warehouse

Primary Objective: Inventory accuracy and logistical efficiency.

  • Precision Inventory: Cycle counting, item lot maintenance, and real-time stock valuation.
  • Logistics: Packing Matrix, internal material movement tracking (Production Receipts, Consumable Requests), and aging period definitions.
  • Infrastructure: Hierarchy-based warehouse structure (Types, Locations, Bins) and multi-unit conversion rules.
