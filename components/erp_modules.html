03

Nawras ERP Core Modules

The core of Nawras ERP covers the entire value chain of enterprise operations, from initial customer inquiry to final delivery and financial reconciliation.

Sales Management

Operations & Lifecycle

  • Sales Order & Quotation: Complete lifecycle management with versioning and professional conversion tools
  • Rework Order: Specialized handling for defective products and customer returns
  • Customer Management: Centralized profile, credit, and contact management
  • Order & Quotation History: Comprehensive audit trails

Customer Portal

  • Order Tracker timeline with clear state visualization and progress history
  • Read/unread tracking for customer engagement monitoring
  • Automated item mapping for seamless order processing

Configuration & Analytics

  • Pricing: Flexible "Prices by Customer" configuration for negotiated contracts
  • Sales Force: Management of Sales Representatives, commission structures, and territories
  • Approvals: Multi-level approval matrix for financial controls
  • Sales Analysis: Interactive dashboards for revenue trends and regional performance

Procurement (Purchase)

Suppliers Portal

  • Request for Quotation Management System: Complete RFQ lifecycle management from creation through publishing
  • Proposed Purchase Order Management: Comprehensive system for proposed orders before conversion to discrete POs
  • Discrete Purchase Order Management: Full discrete PO management for confirmed orders
  • Advance Ship Notice Management: Complete ASN system with comprehensive status tracking and warehouse integration
  • Supplier Item Dictionary: Centralized mapping of customer items to supplier-specific codes
  • Shipping Track Management: Automatic recording of all status changes for advance ship notices

Purchase Operations

  • Purchase Order: Robust creation and tracking
  • Requisitions: Internal request workflows to standardize demand
  • Inquiries: Efficient supplier inquiry and quote gathering
  • Goods Receipt: Validation and recording of received materials
  • Proposed PO: AI/System-recommended orders to optimize stock levels

Integration

  • Event-driven integration with Purchase system for automatic RFQ and PO creation
  • Bidirectional integration with Warehouse for receipt notice status updates
  • Notification system for real-time supplier alerts

Manufacturing

Operations & Execution

  • Production Orders: Creation, release, and management of build orders
  • Order Management: Tools for rescheduling and monitoring active jobs
  • Execution & Closing: Recording output, consumption, and finalizing costs

Configuration & Setup

  • Foundation: Work Centers, Machines, and Standard Tasks
  • Product Definition: Bills of Materials (BOMs) and Routing Codes
  • Planning: Production Calendars and item-specific production attributes

Costing & Analytics

  • Costing Engine: Setup of Cost Components, Surcharges, and Operation Rates
  • Calculation: Automated standard and actual cost calculations
  • Cost Analysis: Detailed variances for Materials, Tasks, and overall Cost Summaries
  • Capacity: Real-time visibility into Available Resource Capacity

Inventory & Warehouse

Operations

  • Transactions: Adjustments, Transfers, Goods Receipts, and Deliveries
  • Internal Flow: Production Receipts, Material Requests, and Consumable Requests
  • Accuracy: Cycle Counting and Maintain Item Lots modules
  • Logistics: Packing Matrix and Movement tracking

Configuration

  • Structure: Warehouse Types, Locations, and Bins
  • Classification: Product Types, Classes, Lines, and Commodities
  • Item Master: Comprehensive management of Items, Groups, and Manufacturers
  • Units: Flexible Unit Categories and Conversion rules

KPIs & Analytics

  • KPI Dashboard: Real-time Indicators, Charts, and Alert systems
  • Stock Reports: Real-time Stock Levels, Aging Analysis, Movement reports
  • Valuation: Inventory Valuation reports covering various costing methods
