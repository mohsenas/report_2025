6. 2025 Development Roadmap & Timeline

A detailed chronological breakdown of technical milestones throughout the 2025 development cycle.

JANUARY - FEBRUARY

Financial Integrity & Dimensions

  • Launched Trial Balance, P&L, and Cash Flow reports.
  • Created SanedFinancialPeriods and SanedFinancialDimensions modules.
  • Implemented gRPC services for dimension management and period configurations.
MARCH - APRIL

Expression Engine & Ledger Mapping

  • Enhanced Filter Parser and added the Group By Aggregator for dynamic reporting.
  • Established comprehensive Ledger Mapping configuration system.
  • Integrated ForeignName fields for enhanced multilingual support.
MAY - JUNE

System Integration & Data Seeding

  • Resolved API Gateway lookups for financial entities.
  • Enhanced gRPC structure conversion for complex objects.
  • Optimized geographic data seeding for internationalization.
JULY - AUGUST

AI Agents & Subsidiary Management

  • Established the AppAIAgent framework infrastructure.
  • Implemented the Payable Subsidiary management module.
  • Integrated reference ID functionality into journal entries.
SEPTEMBER - OCTOBER

Supply Chain & Document Processing

  • Launched the Suppliers Portal (RFQ, ASN, POS modules).
  • Integrated the bilingual OCR system for invoice parsing.
  • Initiated background job processing for high-volume transactions.
NOVEMBER - DECEMBER

Compliance & Modernization

  • Finalized ZATCA Phase 2 integration and reporting engine.
  • Completed global migration to Angular 20.
  • Deployed the cross-module Audit Logging Service.
