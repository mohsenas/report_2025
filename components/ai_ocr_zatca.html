3. Specialized Intelligence & Compliance

Nawras has evolved beyond a traditional ERP by integrating artificial intelligence and rigorous regulatory compliance features specific to the KSA market.

3.1 AppAIAgent Platform

Established a comprehensive AI agent framework that evolved throughout the year:

  • Specialized Agents: Sales Approval (trend analysis), Purchase Approval (supplier evaluation), Inventory Insights (stock optimization), and Risk Scoring.
  • Prescriptive Systems: Lead time prediction using machine learning models and multi-tenant model training.
  • Auto-Matching: AI-driven supplier invoice matching with document processing.

3.2 Bilingual OCR System

Implemented advanced OCR capabilities supporting both Arabic and English text extraction:

  • Integration: Seamlessly integrated with Tesseract OCR engine and ImageMagick for image preprocessing.
  • Capabilities: Full support for invoice parsing, PDF text extraction, and automated metadata generation.
  • Infrastructure: Dockerized environment for critical dependencies (Ghostscript, Leptonica).

3.3 ZATCA E-Invoicing Integration

Full Phase 2 compliance for the Saudi Tax Authority (ZATCA):

  • EGS Unit Management: Complete lifecycle management for electronic generation units, including registration and onboarding code handling.
  • Reporting Engine: Automatic reporting of invoices/credit notes with QR code generation, ICV management, and PIH chain validation.
  • Security: Advanced certificate management and environment protection (Simulation vs. Production).
