AI & Intelligent Automation

Revolutionizing ERP capabilities through advanced AI agents and document intelligence.

AppAIAgent Framework

Established a comprehensive AI agent framework with gRPC services. Specialized agents for Sales trends, Purchase performance, and Inventory optimization.

Advanced OCR System

Bilingual (Arabic/English) text extraction service supporting PDF and images. Integrated with Tesseract and ImageMagick for high-fidelity invoice parsing.

Predictive Analytics

Implemented lead time prediction models and risk scoring agents for supply chain resilience and proactive decision-making.

Financial Excellence & Compliance

Driving financial integrity with ZATCA-compliant e-invoicing and robust reporting engines.

Fully compliant integration with the Saudi Tax Authority. Automated submission of standard and simplified e-invoices, QR code generation, and ICV/PIH chain validation.

Advanced Reporting

Dynamic P&L, Balance Sheet, and Trial Balance reports with Excel export. Period-based filtering and multi-dimensional financial analysis.

Transaction Core

Modernized Journal Entries, General Ledger management, and background transaction processing for high-volume financial data.
