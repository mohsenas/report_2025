Executive Summary

The 2025 fiscal year marks a transformative period for Obeikan Digital Solutions, highlighted by the rapid evolution of the Nawras ERP system. Kitsys for Digital Solutions has successfully delivered a robust, integrated enterprise solution addressing complex business needs across sales, procurement, manufacturing, finance, and logistics.

AI agents automatically detect, analyze, and explain anomalies and exceptions in business processes. Provides clear, actionable insights for decision-makers.

Auto-Approval

Intelligent auto-approval system for routine transactions based on configurable business rules. Reduces manual processing time while maintaining control.

Planning Intelligence

AI-powered planning agents optimize resource allocation, scheduling, and inventory management using predictive analytics and machine learning models.
